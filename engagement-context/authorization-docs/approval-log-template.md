## Approval quality checklist

An approval should include:

- asset or asset class;
- environment;
- allowed test class;
- authorized tester;
- start and end time;
- rate or safety limits where relevant;
- data-handling limits;
- explicit exclusions;
- emergency stop contact.

Do not accept vague approvals such as:

```text
Test whatever you find.
Do a full attack.
Try to break everything.
Use any method.
```
