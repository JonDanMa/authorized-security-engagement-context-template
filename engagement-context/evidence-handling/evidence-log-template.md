## Evidence acceptance checklist

- [ ] No secrets.
- [ ] No customer data unless explicitly approved and minimized.
- [ ] No unnecessary raw logs.
- [ ] No unrelated target data.
- [ ] Timestamp included.
- [ ] Asset and environment included.
- [ ] Finding linked to a report.
- [ ] Remediation owner identified.
